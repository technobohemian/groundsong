4-step playbook
01 Warm outreach first
Leverage existing corporate and NFP networks before any cold approach. Do not cold-sell before exhausting warm channels.
02 Lead with the problem
"We help organisations meet RAP commitments through meaningful, culturally grounded wellbeing programs." Short. Specific. Relevant.
03 Offer a pilot session
60–90 min at $1,500–$2,000. Framed as a pilot for their internal wellbeing program. Goal: convert to retainer within 60 days.
04 Push toward retainers
Target 6-session annual contracts from the first proposal. Retainers stabilise cash flow and deepen the client relationship.
